# Code of Conduct

Be kind. Be specific. Assume good faith. Disagree productively about technical choices; don't make it personal.

That standard applies everywhere this project's community interacts — issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions. Harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory behavior are not tolerated in any form.

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, and contributions that violate this standard, and may block repeat offenders. To report a violation, email wessamfathi@gmail.com with `[suhail conduct]` in the subject; reports are handled confidentially.
